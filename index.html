Frequently Asked Questions

Is Pawcket free to use?

Pawcket offers a free tier with basic features. Premium features are available with a subscription for advanced health tracking and community features.

How does the AI Pet Expert work?

Our AI analyzes your pet's health records, behavior patterns, and your specific questions to provide personalized advice. Ask anything about nutrition, training, health concerns, or general care.

How does the Smart Pet Matching work?

Our AI analyzes your pet's breed, age, personality, energy level, and location to find compatible playmates and adoption opportunities. We consider factors like temperament compatibility, size matching, and geographic proximity to ensure successful connections.

What makes adoptions and events "verified"?

We partner with trusted shelters, rescue organizations, and event organizers. All listings are vetted for authenticity, and we maintain strict standards to ensure safe, responsible pet care practices.

How does the Visible Pet feature work?

Connect with pet cameras, wearables, or smart devices to monitor your pet's activity, behavior, and health metrics in real-time. Get alerts for unusual behavior and stay connected even when you're away.
